◆ kore_mixer_mix()

KORE_FUNC void kore_mixer_mix ( kore_audio_buffer *  buffer,
uint32_t  samples 
)

Mixes audio into the provided buffer. kore_mixer_init sets this as the audio-callback but you can also call it manually to mix with your own audio. To do that, first call kore_mixer_init, then call kore_audio_set_callback to set it to your own callback and call kore_mixer_mix from within that callback. Please be aware that the callback will run in a separate audio-thread.

Parameters
bufferThe audio-buffer to be filled
samplesThe number of samples to be filled in
